About Me

Student & Artist

My name is Wendy, and I am 19 years old. My birthday is December 13, 2002, i currently studying at URS Antipolo taking the course of Computer Engineering.
I loved to draw when I was young, it started as a hobby until drawing became my passion. Right now, my hobby is playing Genshin Impact and watching some programming stuff to learned more about my course. I have four pet cats and I treat them like my children. My favorite season is the rainy season, especially when I do a lot of drawing, It's relaxing to draw on a rainy day.

Journey

How i started as Artist

I started to draw when I was a child. I used to draw with crayons. I also drew my dolls until I drew my stuffed toy, Hello Kitty. That's when my passion for drawing began. When I started in kindergarten, I often drew on the back of my notebook that i used at school. My mom used to get mad at me because instead of studying, I was doing sketches. I wasted a lot of paper before I got better, but I don't regret it Because of those papers, I learned and got better at drawing. Because of the artwork, I have an income and I spend it on the things I want and my cats still need. When i was in 9th grade i met a batchmate that has a skill in coloring, i saw her first at the social media, she always posted her works in there and she gain a lot of friends and that is how i met her. i chat to her and ask some advice about how she colored her works, she taught me well and i practice her style until i make my own style or way of coloring. After i master traditional way of drawing in 10th grade i practice the digital way. i try many style until i found my style i always try and try. The secret of the beautiful piece is the hardworking artist.
